## ✨ Features

### 🎓 Academic Management
- ✅ Complete Student Lifecycle Management
- ✅ Faculty & Staff Management
- ✅ Smart Attendance with shortage alerts
- ✅ Timetable Builder (click-to-add)
- ✅ Examination Scheduling & Results
- ✅ Hall Ticket Generation

### 💰 Finance
- ✅ Fee Structure Setup
- ✅ Fee Collection with receipts
- ✅ Payment history & reports
- ✅ Monthly collection charts

### 📚 Facilities
- ✅ Library with Issue/Return/Fine
- ✅ Hostel Room Management
- ✅ Room Allocation & Vacating

### 📊 Reports & Analytics
- ✅ Year-wise student charts
- ✅ Fee collection overview
- ✅ Gender & category distribution
- ✅ CSV export for all modules
- ✅ Print-friendly layouts

### 🔒 System
- ✅ Role-based login (Admin/Faculty/Student/Accounts/Librarian)
- ✅ **JSON Backup & Restore** (cross-device support)
- ✅ Auto-backup every 24 hours
- ✅ Dark/Light theme toggle
- ✅ PWA — Install as App
- ✅ Offline support (Service Worker)
- ✅ Mobile responsive
